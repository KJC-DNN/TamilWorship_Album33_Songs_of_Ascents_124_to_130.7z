Album 33 Song 2
Trust in the Lord – Psalm 125.
Those who trust in the Lord are as secure as Mount Zion and shall not be moved. Hallelujah!

Study Note
Psalm 125 encourages believers to put their trust in the Lord. Those who trust Him are as secure and unshakable as Mount Zion, surrounded by God’s protection, just as the mountains surround Jerusalem.

Prayer

English:
Lord Jesus, help me trust in You fully. Make me like Mount Zion — firm, secure, and unmoved. Amen. Hallelujah!

Tamil:
கர்த்தாவே இயேசு, உம்மீது முழு நம்பிக்கை வைக்க எனக்கு உதவும். என்னை சீயோன் மலையைப் போல உறுதியானவனாக்கும். ஆமென். அல்லேலூயா!
